Arsene Wenger was born on the 22nd of October 1949 in Strasbourg, France. Before he became a manager he was a player. He played for several teams. he started of his youth career at FC Duttlenheim in 1963-1969. Then moved on to Mutzig from 1969- 1973. He then began his senior career and moved onto Mulhhouse where he spent 2 years( 1973 - 1975) after that he moved to ASPV Strasbourg where he spent another 3 years(1975-1978). He then completed his career at RC Strasbourg where he spent the last 3 years of his career, 1978-1981

Arsene began his managerial career at Nancy - Lorraine, managing here for 3 years , 1984-1987. After this he moved onto AS Monaco this is when he began to win trophies, this spell lasted from 1987- 1994 here Ligue 1 was won. During the 1987-88 season, following this success the Coupe de France was won in 1990-91. Following the success at Monaco Arsene decided to take on the challenge of Nagoya Grampus where he continued to win trophies. In his two year spell he won 2 trophies he managed the club from 1994-1996 and won the Emperor's Cup in 1995 and the J league super cup in 1996.

Finally in 1996 Arsene joyed the club he was destined to manage due to the obvious similarity in his name Arsenal. Arsene Wenger has led Arsenal to several major honours, the list never ending. Arsenal have won 3 Premier Leagues under Wenge( 1997-98,2001-02,2003-04).
